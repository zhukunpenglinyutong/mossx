- Slice C stalled / runtime protection: Codex no-progress settlement in `useThreadEventHandlers.ts`, stop-after-stall unblock behavior in `useThreadMessaging.ts`, generation-aware active-work/runtime-ended guards in `src-tauri/src/runtime/**` and `src-tauri/src/backend/**`.
